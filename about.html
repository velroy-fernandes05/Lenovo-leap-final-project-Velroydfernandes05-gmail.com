About AI Study Assistant

This project is developed under SDG 4 (Quality Education). The aim is to help students access learning resources, practice quizzes, and get AI-based educational support.

Project Objectives

  • Improve learning efficiency
  • Provide AI-powered guidance
  • Recommend study resources
  • Track learning progress
